Colour Code

  • Black - Planned
  • Black - Rumour/Uncertain
  • Green - Released at least 1 episode
  • Blue - Simulcasts
  • Strikeout - Dropped

For example, Green Strikeout means that at least one episode has been released and it has been dropped.
